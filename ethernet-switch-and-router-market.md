<p><p>An Ethernet switch and router are essential networking devices that enable communication between devices on a local area network (LAN) and connect different networks, respectively. An Ethernet switch operates at the data link layer, connecting multiple devices within a network, allowing them to communicate efficiently by forwarding data only to the intended destination. In contrast, a router operates at the network layer, directing data packets between different networks, commonly used for connecting a LAN to the Internet.</p><p>The Ethernet Switch and Router Market is poised for substantial growth, driven by increasing demand for high-speed connectivity, the proliferation of IoT devices, and the expansion of cloud services. The growing emphasis on data-centric applications and rising enterprise networking needs are major contributors to this market expansion.
