<p><strong>The Smart Film Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic Smart Glass</li><li>SPD Smart Glass</li><li>PDLC Smart Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart film glass market comprises three main types: electrochromic, suspended particle device (SPD), and polymer dispersed liquid crystal (PDLC) smart glass. Electrochromic smart glass changes its tint in response to electrical signals, offering energy-efficient light control. SPD smart glass utilizes suspended particles that align when an electric current is applied, instantly altering transparency. PDLC smart glass combines liquid crystal technology, enabling it to switch between transparent and opaque states, providing privacy and glare reduction. Each type enhances comfort and aesthetics in various applications.</p></p>
