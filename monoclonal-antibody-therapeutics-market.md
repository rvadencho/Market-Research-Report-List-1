<p><p>The monoclonal antibody therapeutics market includes various types, primarily humanized antibodies and human-mouse chimeric antibodies. Humanized antibodies are engineered from mouse antibodies, retaining only essential human components to minimize immune response, making them safer and more effective for therapy. In contrast, human-mouse chimeric antibodies combine mouse and human antibody sequences, offering a mixed approach that can enhance binding affinity and specificity. Both types are pivotal in treating various diseases, especially cancers and autoimmune disorders, by targeting specific antigens.</p></p>
