<p><p>The Desktop Passport Reader (DPR) market comprises several types, including Magnetic Stripe Interface (MSI), Radio Frequency Identification (RFID), Optical Character Recognition (OCR), and Others. MSI readers rely on magnetic stripes for data retrieval, while RFID uses radio waves for wireless communication with passports. OCR technology scans and interprets printed text to process passport information. The "Others" category encompasses various technologies and solutions not fitting neatly into these main types, contributing to the diverse capabilities of desktop passport readers in various applications.</p></p>
