<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive green tires market encompasses two primary types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature layers of fabric that run diagonally, offering durability and flexibility, making them suitable for specific applications like vintage vehicles and agricultural equipment. In contrast, radial tires have steel belts running perpendicular to the tread, providing enhanced fuel efficiency, better traction, and improved handling. The radial design is preferred for modern vehicles due to its performance advantages and lower rolling resistance, contributing to overall sustainability in automotive applications.</p></p>
