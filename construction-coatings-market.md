<p><strong>The Construction Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Borne</li><li>Solvent Borne</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction coatings market is categorized into three main types: waterborne, solventborne, and powder coatings. Waterborne coatings utilize water as the primary solvent, offering low VOC emissions and easy cleanup. Solventborne coatings use organic solvents, providing strong adhesion and durability but with higher VOC content. Powder coatings are applied as a dry powder, cured under heat, and are known for their durability and environmental benefits, as they emit little to no volatile organic compounds during application. Each type serves specific application needs in construction.</p></p>
