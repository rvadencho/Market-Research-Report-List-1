<p><p>The Low Speed Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB) system market comprises various types, including Forward Collision Warning (FCW), Dynamic Brake Support (DBS), and Crash Imminent Braking (CIB). FCW alerts drivers of potential collisions, enhancing situational awareness. DBS assists in braking by providing additional support when a crash is anticipated, while CIB activates the brakes automatically if a collision is deemed unavoidable. Together, these systems aim to enhance vehicle safety and mitigate impacts in low-speed driving scenarios.</p></p>
