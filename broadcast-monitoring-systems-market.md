<p><ul><li>Composite Video Monitoring</li><li>S-Video Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Broadcast monitoring systems encompass various market types, including composite video monitoring and S-video monitoring. Composite video monitoring utilizes a single cable to transmit video signals, simplifying connections but offering lower image quality. In contrast, S-video monitoring separates brightness and color signals, resulting in improved picture quality with less interference. Both methods are essential for ensuring the integrity of broadcast content, allowing media professionals to assess transmission quality and compliance with standards in real-time.</p></p>
