<p><ul><li>Discrete Cloud-Based Simulation Software</li><li>Continuous Cloud-Based Simulation Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cloud-based simulation software market can be categorized into two main types: discrete and continuous. Discrete cloud-based simulation software focuses on modeling systems with distinct events occurring at separate points in time, making it ideal for applications like manufacturing and logistics. In contrast, continuous cloud-based simulation software deals with systems that evolve over time in a fluid manner, such as in environmental modeling or physics. Both types leverage cloud infrastructure for scalability, flexibility, and enhanced collaboration among users.</p></p>
