<p><p>Indium Tin Oxide (ITO) coatings are categorized into high purity and low purity types based on their composition and application. High purity ITO coatings, containing over 90% indium oxide, are utilized in advanced electronics, displays, and touchscreens, ensuring optimal conductivity and transparency. In contrast, low purity ITO coatings, with a lower indium oxide concentration, are more cost-effective and commonly used in less demanding applications, such as basic shielding and optical devices. Each type serves distinct market needs based on performance and cost.</p></p>
