<p><p>The heavy-duty loading ramps market features two primary types: hydraulic and pneumatic ramps. Hydraulic ramps utilize fluid pressure to facilitate lifting and lowering loads, offering precise control and stability, making them ideal for heavy and large items. Pneumatic ramps, on the other hand, operate using compressed air to achieve similar functions, providing quick adjustments and often lighter construction. Both types cater to various industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and safety in loading and unloading operations across diverse sectors.</p></p>
