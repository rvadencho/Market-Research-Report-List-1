<p><ul><li>Fat-Soluble Vitamins</li><li>Water Soluble Vitamins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The monomeric vitamins market is divided into two main types: fat-soluble and water-soluble vitamins. Fat-soluble vitamins, such as A, D, E, and K, dissolve in fats and oils, making them essential for various bodily functions, including vision, immunity, and bone health. Water-soluble vitamins, including B-complex vitamins and vitamin C, dissolve in water and play crucial roles in energy production, metabolism, and maintaining healthy skin and nerve function. Both categories cater to distinct consumer needs and health benefits.</p></p>
