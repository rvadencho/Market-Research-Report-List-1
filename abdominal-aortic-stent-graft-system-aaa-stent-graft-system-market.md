<p><p>The Abdominal Aortic Stent Graft System is designed to treat abdominal aortic aneurysms, offering minimally invasive options for vascular surgeons. The market is categorized into two main types: unibody and bifurcated stent grafts. Unibody stent grafts consist of a single continuous structure, ideal for straight aorta configurations. In contrast, bifurcated stent grafts feature two branches, accommodating the body's anatomy by addressing both the aorta and its iliac arteries, making them suitable for more complex vascular conditions.</p></p>
