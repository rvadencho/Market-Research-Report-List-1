<p><p>The asthma therapeutics market comprises three primary types: inhalation, parenteral, and oral. Inhalation therapies, such as metered-dose inhalers and nebulizers, deliver medication directly to the lungs for quick relief and control. Parenteral therapies involve injections to manage severe asthma or allergic reactions, offering rapid systemic effects. Oral therapies, including tablets and capsules, provide a convenient option for long-term management but may have slower onset compared to inhaled routes. Each type addresses specific patient needs and treatment goals.</p></p>
