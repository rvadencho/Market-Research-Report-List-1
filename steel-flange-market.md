<p><p>The steel flange market comprises various types, including weld flanges, blind flanges, slip-on flanges, and others. Weld flanges are designed for strong connections through welding, offering robust structural integrity. Blind flanges seal off systems without a connection point, preventing fluid or gas leakage. Slip-on flanges allow easy alignment and fitting over pipes, enhancing installation convenience. Other types include threaded and lap joint flanges, each serving specific applications across industries like oil and gas, construction, and manufacturing.</p></p>
