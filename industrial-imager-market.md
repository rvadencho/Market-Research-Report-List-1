<p><p>The industrial imager market encompasses applications in both contact and non-contact measurement, essential for quality control and automation in manufacturing. Contact measurement involves direct interaction with the object, ensuring precise dimensional assessments, often used in mechanical and engineering processes. Both methods enhance efficiency, accuracy, and safety in various industrial applications.</p></p>
