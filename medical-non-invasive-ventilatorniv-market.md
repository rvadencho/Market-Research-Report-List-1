<p><p>The non-invasive ventilator (NIV) market primarily includes CPAP and BiPAP ventilators. Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) ventilators deliver a steady airflow to maintain open airways, primarily used for sleep apnea and respiratory conditions. Bi-level Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) ventilators provide varying levels of air pressure for inhalation and exhalation, catering to patients with different respiratory needs. Both types are essential for improving patient comfort and reducing the need for invasive procedures, thus promoting effective management of respiratory disorders.</p></p>
