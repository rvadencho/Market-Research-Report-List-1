<p><p>The Construction Adhesive Tapes market can be categorized into three main types: Hot-Melt Based, Solvent-Based, and Others. Hot-Melt-Based tapes use a thermoplastic adhesive that solidifies upon cooling, providing strong bonding for various materials. Solvent-Based tapes utilize adhesives dissolved in solvents, offering excellent adhesion and flexibility. The "Others" category encompasses specialty tapes with unique formulations, such as double-sided or pressure-sensitive tapes, tailored for specific applications in construction, packaging, and automotive industries, enhancing versatility and performance.</p></p>
