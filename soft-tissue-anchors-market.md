<p><p>Soft tissue anchors are devices used in orthopedic and surgical procedures to secure soft tissues, such as ligaments and tendons, to bone. Additionally, the development of innovative anchor designs and improved biocompatibility has enhanced surgical outcomes, boosting the market.</p><p>Recent trends include the rising preference for minimally invasive surgical procedures, which reduce recovery time and scarring. Moreover, technological innovations such as the integration of bioactive coatings on anchors to promote healing are gaining traction.
