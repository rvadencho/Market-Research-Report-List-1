<p><p>The biometric software market encompasses various authentication methods, including Single Factor Authentication (SFA), Two Factor Authentication (2FA), and Multi Factor Authentication (MFA). SFA relies solely on one biometric trait, such as a fingerprint. In contrast, 2FA combines one biometric trait with another authentication method, enhancing security. MFA offers even greater protection by requiring multiple biometric traits or additional factors, like passwords or tokens. Each type addresses different security needs, catering to varying levels of risk and user convenience.</p></p>
