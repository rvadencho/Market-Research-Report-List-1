<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle Range Extender market consists primarily of two types: water-cooled and air-cooled range extenders. Water-cooled range extenders utilize a liquid cooling system, offering efficient thermal management and enhancing overall performance, particularly under high loads. In contrast, air-cooled range extenders rely on air circulation for cooling, simplifying design and reducing weight but may have limitations in extreme temperature conditions. Both types serve to extend the driving range of electric vehicles, improving their practicality and appeal to consumers.</p></p>
