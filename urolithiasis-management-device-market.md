<p><p>The urolithiasis management device market consists of several types aimed at treating kidney stones. Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL) utilizes high-energy shock waves to break stones externally. Intracorporeal lithotripsy involves using lasers or ultrasound within the urinary tract to fragment stones. Percutaneous nephrolithotomy is a minimally invasive surgical procedure that removes large stones through the skin. Other methods may include medical management and newer technologies. Each approach offers varying levels of invasiveness and effectiveness depending on the stone's size and location.</p></p>
