<p><strong>The Digital Gloss Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-angle</li><li>Dual Angles</li><li>Multi-angles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital gloss meter market is categorized into three types based on measurement angles: single-angle, dual-angle, and multi-angle gloss meters. Single-angle gloss meters measure surface gloss at one specific angle, providing a quick assessment of gloss levels. Dual-angle gloss meters offer measurements at two angles, giving a more comprehensive view of gloss and reflection properties. Multi-angle gloss meters measure gloss across several angles, allowing for detailed analysis of surface textures and finishes, making them suitable for diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
