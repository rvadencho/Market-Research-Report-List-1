<p><p>The Clay Nanopowder market is primarily categorized into two types: Kaolinite and Smectite. Kaolinite, known for its high purity and fine particle size, is extensively used in ceramics, paper coatings, and cosmetics. Smectite, characterized by its swelling properties, often finds applications in pharmaceuticals, oil drilling, and environmental remediation. Both types exhibit unique physicochemical properties that make them valuable in various industrial applications, driving the demand for clay nanopowder across multiple sectors.</p></p>
