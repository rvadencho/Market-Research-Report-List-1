<p><p>Urology Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are essential medical devices used for surgical procedures, utilizing high-frequency electrical currents to cut tissue and coagulate blood. The market includes three main types: monopole electrosurgical units, which employ a single active electrode and require a grounding pad; bipolar electrosurgical units, featuring two electrodes that work together for precision in confined areas; and vessel sealing units, which securely close blood vessels and tissues, minimizing bleeding and enhancing surgical efficiency.</p></p>
