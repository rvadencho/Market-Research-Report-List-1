<p><p>Alloyed steel seamless pipes are categorized into two main types: cold drawn and hot rolled. Cold drawn pipes undergo a process where they are pulled through a die at room temperature, resulting in a tighter tolerance and enhanced mechanical properties. Hot rolled pipes, on the other hand, are formed by heating the steel above its recrystallization temperature, allowing for easier shaping and lower production costs. Both types cater to diverse applications based on strength, durability, and precision requirements.</p></p>
