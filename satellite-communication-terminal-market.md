S Band is used for mobile communication and some radar applications. X Band is favored for military and weather services due to its reliability. Ku Band supports high-throughput data transmission, commonly used in broadcasting and internet services. Ka Band offers higher frequencies for enhanced data capacity, ideal for advanced broadband applications. Other bands may include V and L Bands, used for specialized communications.</p></p>
