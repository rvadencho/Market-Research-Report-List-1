<p><p>Composite pipes in the new energy market, including clad pipes and non-metal composite pipes, offer enhanced performance for various applications. Clad pipes feature a metal layer bonded to a non-metallic substrate, providing durability and corrosion resistance while maintaining flexibility. Non-metal composite pipes, on the other hand, are made entirely from composite materials, offering advantages such as lightweight design, resistance to electrolytic corrosion, and reduced thermal conductivity, making them ideal for transporting fluids in challenging environments.</p></p>
