<p><p>Food safety testing systems and services can be categorized into traditional and rapid market types. Traditional methods rely on established laboratory techniques, often requiring longer processing times for results, leading to delayed decision-making. These methods typically include culture-based assays and biochemical tests. In contrast, rapid market methods utilize advanced technologies such as PCR, immunoassays, and biosensors, providing quicker results, often within hours. This enables faster response to contamination risks, enhancing food safety and compliance in the supply chain.</p></p>
