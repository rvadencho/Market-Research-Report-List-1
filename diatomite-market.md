<p><ul><li>Anhydrous Substance</li><li>Flux Calcined</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diatomite market is segmented into two main types: anhydrous substance and flux calcined. Anhydrous diatomite, consisting primarily of silica, is used for filtration, absorbents, and as a lightweight filler in various industries. Flux calcined diatomite undergoes a heat treatment process that enhances its structural integrity and thermal resistance, making it suitable for applications in ceramics, construction, and insulation materials. Both types serve essential roles across diverse sectors, driven by their unique properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
