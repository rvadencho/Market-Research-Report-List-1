<p><ul><li>Single Acting</li><li>Double-Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydro-cylinder market is categorized into single-acting and double-acting types. Single-acting cylinders utilize hydraulic pressure to move in one direction, relying on a spring or gravity for retraction, making them simpler and cost-effective for lightweight applications. In contrast, double-acting cylinders harness hydraulic force for both extension and retraction, providing greater power and control for heavy-duty tasks. This versatility makes double-acting cylinders essential for various industrial applications, while single-acting options are ideal for simpler operations.</p></p>
