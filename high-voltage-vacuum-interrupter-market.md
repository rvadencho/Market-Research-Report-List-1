<p><p>The High Voltage Vacuum Interrupter market includes two main types: Embedded Type and Knob Type. Embedded Type interrupters are integrated into circuit breakers or switches, optimizing space and enhancing insulation. They are widely used in compact electrical systems. In contrast, Knob Type interrupters feature a manual operation mechanism that allows for easy maintenance and testing. Both types are essential in ensuring reliable electrical isolation and protection in high voltage applications, catering to diverse industry needs and preferences.</p></p>
