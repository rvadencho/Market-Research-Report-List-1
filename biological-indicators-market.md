<p><p>Biological indicators are vital tools in sterilization processes, ensuring that conditions are adequate for microbial eradication. The self-contained biological indicator market involves units that incorporate a microbial population and a growth medium in a single package, simplifying usage and monitoring. In contrast, biological indicator strips consist of a separate spore strip that is exposed during the sterilization cycle and subsequently cultured to check for microbial growth. Both types provide essential validation for sterility assurance in healthcare and laboratory settings.</p></p>
