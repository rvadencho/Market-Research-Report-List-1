<p><p>The high voltage connector market in automobiles is segmented into three main types: line to line, line to board, and board to board. Line to line connectors facilitate direct electrical connections between two cables, ensuring efficient power transfer. Line to board connectors connect cables to circuit boards, enabling integration of components. Board to board connectors facilitate connections between multiple circuit boards within the vehicle, supporting complex electrical systems. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing the safety and performance of electric and hybrid vehicles.</p></p>
